Chocolate Cake

Description

Your classic old delicious chocolate cake! Yum!

Ingredients

  • Devil's Food Cake Mix
  • Instant Chocolate Pudding Mix
  • Sour Cream
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Warm Water
  • Large Eggs
  • Semisweet Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and grease a 12-cup Bundt pan generously
  2. Beat cake mix, pudding mix, sour cream, oil, water, and eggs in a large bowl until smooth and well blended together
  3. Stir in the chocolate chips
  4. Pour batter into the prepared Bundt pan
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is springy to the touch and a toothpick that's inserted at the center of the cake comes out clean (50 to 55 minutes)
