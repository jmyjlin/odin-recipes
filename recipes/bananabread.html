Banana Bread

Description

This is some tasty banana bread! Great way to use those outdated bananas!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Beat brown sugar and butter with a mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in the eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir the banana mixture into flour mixture until it has just combined.
  3. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick that's inserted into the center come sout clean (takes about 60 minutes)
  5. Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn it onto a wire rack to cool completely.
