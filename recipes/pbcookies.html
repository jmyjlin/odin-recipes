Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

This recipe outputs a delicious peanut buttery cookie! Perfect for sharing with the whole family!

  • Peanut butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Mix peanut butter, sugar, and egg all in a bowl using a mixer until it's smooth and creamy
  3. Roll dough into 1-inch balls and place them 2 inches apart on an ungreased baking sheet
  4. Flatten each cookie with a fork to make a criss-cross pattern
  5. Bake for about 10 minutes
